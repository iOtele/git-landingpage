HOW IT WORKS

What is an eSIM?
An eSIM (Embedded SIM) is a digital SIM card built into a device, allowing users to activate a mobile network without needing a physical SIM card. It enables users to switch carriers or activate plans directly from their device, making it convenient for travel.

ACTIVATE YOUR ESIM IN MINUTES

  1. Check your device compatibility
  2. Obtain the eSIM QR code
  3. Scan the QR code once cellular / network settings, after selecting “Add eSIM”.
  4. Confirm the activation
  5. Complete the setup once eSIM is added
